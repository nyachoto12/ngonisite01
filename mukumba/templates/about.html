ABOUT MCC

11 years upto now, Mukumba Ceilings has successfully expanded its operation . Since our establishement in 2008, Mukumba Ceilings has established and grown to be a diverse company providing local and national clients with high quality products and excellent workmanship. Mukumba Ceilings offers a complete range of products for skimmed ceiling material and all other accessories. We are also experts on using any kind of materials for backslashing,ceilings and walls shinning.
